> new user onboarding & faq_

> the basics_

welcome to greg. it's a private media ecosystem built on unraid and plex. it's not always stable, but it's always free.

Q: how do i get access? A: typically by invite. if you have a link to the registration portal (wizarr), use it. otherwise, contact thomas.

> streaming_

Q: what apps should i use? A: the official plex app is available on smart TVs, apple TV, nvidia shield, and mobile. avoid the web browser player if possible; it causes "transcoding" which kills the server.

Q: it's buffering. why? A: 99% of the time, it's your internet or your settings. in the plex app, set Remote Quality to "Original" or "Maximum". greg has a big pipe; use it.

> requests_

Q: how do i ask for new movies/shows? A: use the request link on the main page (Overseerr). log in with your plex account, find your show, and hit request. it's automated from there.

> support_

Q: how can i help? A: running 140TB of disks and a modern CPU isn't cheap on the electric bill. if you use greg daily, consider hitting the venmo/paypal links to keep the lights on and the disks spinning.

Q: something is broken. A: email greg@weirdplexbutok.biz. i check it eventually.
